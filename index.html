Frequently Asked Questions

These are frequently asked questions about the importance of taking care of our teeth.

How often should I brush my teeth?

Brush your teeth at least twice a day—morning and night. For best results, use fluoride toothpaste and a soft-bristled toothbrush.

Is mouthwash necessary?

Mouthwash can help reduce plaque, freshen breath, and provide additional fluoride. It’s not a substitute for brushing and flossing but can be a beneficial addition to your oral hygiene routine.

How often should I replace my toothbrush?

Replace your toothbrush every 3 to 4 months, or sooner if the bristles become frayed. A worn toothbrush is less effective at cleaning your teeth.

What is the best way to clean braces or other dental appliances?

Clean braces or other dental appliances as recommended by your orthodontist or dentist. Typically, this includes brushing more frequently and using special cleaning devices or interdental brushes

Why are regular dental check-ups important?

Regular check-ups allow your dentist to monitor your oral health, catch problems early, and provide professional cleanings that help prevent issues such as cavities and gum disease.
